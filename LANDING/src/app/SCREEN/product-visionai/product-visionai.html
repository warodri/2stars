Vision AI

Recognition technology for real-world interaction, recommendations, and bridge experiences between physical and digital environments.

What it does

Vision AI enables people to scan products, environments, or faces and receive contextual responses, recommendations, or guided next steps.

Why teams use it

It creates memorable interactive experiences while tying recognition and context into measurable business flows and customer journeys.

Typical use cases

Retail discovery, smart recommendations, real-world campaign activations, visual onboarding, and assisted service experiences.
