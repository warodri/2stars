AI Chat

Flat-fee business messaging that automates conversations, triggers workflows, collects payments, and supports customers at scale.

What it does

AI Chat combines automated conversations and workflow execution in one product, without relying on per-message pricing models.

Why teams use it

It offers predictable pricing, faster automation rollout, and a more direct connection between customer interactions and operations.

Typical use cases

Customer support automation, payments follow-up, lead capture, service workflows, FAQ handling, and guided product experiences.
