RelAI

AI-powered messaging flows across WhatsApp, RCS, Telegram, and connected customer channels.

What it does

RelAI lets teams create messaging flows with simple prompts, connect providers quickly, and move from setup to live automation in minutes.

Why teams use it

It shortens launch time, simplifies approvals and onboarding, and creates a practical path from conversational AI to business execution.

Typical use cases

Lead qualification, customer support routing, post-sale messaging, onboarding journeys, reminders, and payment-related communication.
